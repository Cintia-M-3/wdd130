Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help this family bussiness become more accessible to other families, so they can have a moment that they can remember. This company as the purpose of continuing to keep their characteristic and authenticity of being a small family bussines, but at the same time reach out more target audience, becoming more acessible and more modern. This website will also be of great convinience to the user, because will have acess to all information about the specific product that this bussiness as to offer.

Audience

Our audience is families. We are a family bussiness and we know the importance of families and how importante is that they share moments together. Because is a small bussiness, we make affordable prices, independent of the economic situation, all families can spend time together and enjoy nature. In this world were everything is rushed, we barely see our families or have special moments with them. Is important to get away a little bit of the chaos, and enjoy nature and a good fmaily activity. This website will as the purpose of being reached by the user mostly through mobile devices. Is build up thinking of busy parents that want to plan quality time with their kids or sons and daughters that want to suprise their parents
